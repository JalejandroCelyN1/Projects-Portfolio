NYSE bull vs bear forecasting

Objective: estimate bull/bear market scenarios and validate statistical hypotheses for S&P 500 stocks.

Problem and objective

Return patterns and variables linked to extreme market behavior had to be identified to support investment decisions.

My role and contribution

I formulated hypotheses, structured statistical testing, compared period-based outcomes, and synthesized executive findings.

Technologies and tools

Python, Pandas, Numpy, inferential statistics, visualization, and SQL exploratory queries.

Outcomes and insights

During extreme market days, bull cases increased to 62% vs 47% on regular days. Differential behavior by issuer and month was identified.

Learning applied

I strengthened hypothesis validation and business-oriented communication of statistical results.
